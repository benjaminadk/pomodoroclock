Pomodoro Clock

A Pomodoro Clock is a productivity and time management tool used to structure work flow. Also, the user gains a sense of achievement by simply finishing a session. Pomodoro translates to TOMATO from its Italian origin

Instructions

  1. Adjust 'Work Time' slider to desired length of work period (in minutes)
  2. Set 'Break Time' slider to desired break period
  3. Click 'Controls'
  4. 'Play' begins your session
  5. At anytime you may 'Pause' or 'Reset' your session by pressing the coresponding button
  6. Remember: You must set your times before hitting play
  7. Enjoy the show
